Physics is commonly defined as the part of science that deals with the study of matter, it's fundamental constituents, it's motion and behaviour through space and time, and the related entities of energy and force.

Simply put, it's the study of the universe. This field has seen some of the greatest scientists including Einstein, Newton, Schrodinger and many others.

In these two years, you will study the various aspects of physics which include but are not only limited to Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Electromagnetism and Optics.
