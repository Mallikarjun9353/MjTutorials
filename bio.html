Biology in simple terms is the study of all living organisms. It is the natural science that deals with life.

It covers a very broad spectrum that is united by several themes that bond it into a single and coherent field. It is the study of the morphology, physiology, anatomy, behavior, origin and distribution of all living organisms.

This field has seen some eminent figures that include Darwin, Fleming, Leeuwenhoek and many others. In these two years, you will study the branches of Biology which are Botany, Zoology and Medical Science.
