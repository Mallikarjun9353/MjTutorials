Computer Science is the study of computation, automation and information. This field spans theoretical disciplines to practical ones.

Computer science is generally considered more of a research area in comparison to computer programming. Programming involves analysis, algorithm generation, resource computation and implementation of the same.

Distinguished figures such as Alan Turing, Charles Babbage and Lady Ada Lovelace are some of the early computer scientists. In this course, you will be introduced into the basics of computer science and computer programming as well.
